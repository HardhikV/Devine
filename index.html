RP Rules

Basic Rules

1.VDM also known as Vehicle Deathmatch is totally not allowed In RP. VDM means to kill or injure anyone intentionally using any vehicle. As if we kill anyone or hit with the vehicle the person who got hit by the Car will not have any opportunity to save his life. He only has the option to lose his life and no other option is left for him.

2.RDM also known as Random Deathmatch means killing someone with Guns and Weapons without doing any proper RP with him. According to this rule we can not directly kill or shoot anyone without involving in any RP scenario with him. We can not go and directly kill anyone without talking with him and without giving proper time to him to save his life. RDM is totally not allowed in RP.

3.Fail RP is an act of not following RP Rules and performing non-realistic acts in any RP which is nearly impossible in Real Life Scenario. In RP we live the life of our Fictional Character as real life so we can not do any non-realistic things that can not be possible in real life. we have to behave like our character and not use any game mechanics to do Stunts and harmful things that are not possible in our Real Life. According to this rule we have to be in our character and not come out of the character again and again and do non-sense things. Doing these things in RP will be considered as Fail-RP.

4.Fear RP means we should have fear of our life all the time while doing RP. Having Fear of our Life is very necessary in Real LIfe as well as RP because in RP we live the life of our character and we have to take care of our life because every life is precious in RP also.

5.Power Gaming is an advantage in RP scenario by using in-game mechanics. Basically it means when we don't give any chance to the opponent to defend himself and make him self safe from that case and we don't leave any chance to the opponent except losing then it will be considered as Power Gaming which is not allowed In RP.

6.Metagaming is the act when we use information from Outside in the RP without Getting any information about that thing in the RP. If we use any information going out of the character will be considered as Meta Gaming which is strictly Prohibited in the RP. Stream Sniping is also a part of Meta Gaming because in this also we use Information from outside the Game.

7.Combat Logging:- Combat Logging means to log out or disconnect from the game or RP. Like if we are in an RP scenario and we Press 'F8' and quit the game and leave that scenario to save myself then it will be considered as Combat Logging because in real life we can not run away from any scenario.

8.Character Mixing:-Mixing of Two or more character's Knowledge of RP to gain profit or advantage is known as Character Mixing. It means when we use knowledge of one character and to gain advantage in the second character then it is known as Character Mixing.

9.Cop Baiting:- Cop Baiting means involving Cops into a RP scenario again and again without any reason or without creating Proper RP then it is known as COP Baiting.

10.New Life Rule:- When a new life starts, your character will forget all the information and events leading up to your death. However, you are still the same character (same job, gang, friends). New Life Starts when you are taking re-spawn or executed. After respawn you forget 15 minutes(of real life) RP and you can not enter the area of 100m of that situation.

11.Progressive RP:- When you enter the city for the first time you need to explore the city first. You have to make friends and then have to start a journey by knowing the people and city & other kinds of exploration. Also you need to continue your backstory.

12.Supportive RP:- When another player is creating a scenario with you. You have to continue that scenario with that player.

Server Rules

1.Any threats/abuse made to the community or staff will be taken very seriously. A permanent ban will be issued without the chance to appeal.
2.In RP situations, whatever will be the situation, you are not allowed to use and go OOC. Also you are not allowed to go AFK in any active situation
3.When taking hostages, you must provide high-quality role-play. Friendly hostages are not allowed. The negotiating officer cannot be taken as a hostage.
4.Encouraging or forcing other members of the community to break rules is strictly prohibited and will be punished with a ban.
5.Providing false information such as your age when applying for a position/job at our servers or when asked by a staff member is not allowed.
6. While reporting someone, kindly make sure you have sufficient evidence.
7.Roleplaying as a nude character is not allowed. An exception for this rule is if you are inside the strip club (Vanilla Unicorn).
8.If there is no hostage involved in the situation, police can breach in and perform a code-red if required. No Negotiation will be involved in it.
9.If you want to kidnap police and perform legit RP, then you need to do proper RP i.e there should be a minimum 4 police. You have to do RP and gather information whether there are 4 police or not.
10.You are not allowed to camp any locations just to steal or rob someone. This includes legal locations where the majority of the people camp to get free grind
11.Hostage can not be in a situation for more than 15-20 minutes.
12.In any Robbery if the Civilian is a Hostage , if money negotiation is involved then negotiation will be not more than $5000 and if Hostage is LSPD Officer then it will be not more than $10000.
13.Priority and EDM cars are not allowed in robberies and chase situations.
14.When the server is under maintenance, players are expected to not join the server until you are notified. Any player found taking advantage of this or doing exploitation will be immediately taken action upon.
15.If anyone is found using any external tools, software's, add-on, plugins which provide your extra advantage over regular players is considered as exploitation. Any player found exploiting such things will be permanently banned + the players who are involved in the situation can also be banned permanently.
16.Spoilers are not allowed on vehicles going in or getting involved in drive-by attacks.
17. You are not allowed to leave the server from any random spot. You can only leave the server via your motel or house. Only in rare case if your game crashes or you got to do some urgent thing IRL it can be considered but that too if you are in an active it should be reported in #game crash channel on discord.
18.Hunting gun is only to be used to hunt, if used to kill a person, you will be banned permanently without Any Warnings!
19.Exploit of any kind is a bannable offence, report the bug in discord immediately
20.Use of 911 and 311 is prohibited in any active RP situation. If you use it, you will face a temporary ban.
21.OOC spamming in between a situation is not allowed.. You can use /report or open a ticket on discord.
22.No more code red inside Police station. If this happens be ready for the consequences.
23.Doing force RP with other players will not be entertained.
24.You cannot be taken hostage from safezone but they run inside safezone while they are being gun pointed, they will not be considered inside safezone.
25.Not showing fear towards police or gang can be considered as fail of fear RP
26.Use of /me is to show issues you are facing with yourself and not to communicate. Reading out aloud the /me messages is not allowed
27.You may not rob a cop without a valid reason they are not looting cows or your personal armoury. For example: If cops are downed in a shootout after a robbery gone wrong and you ran out of bandages, ammo, armour etc. You may take what you need to continue this scenario but u cant rob them for their guns.
28.Stealing police cars without any Proper RP Scenario or Reason will be considered as Low Effort RP.
29.Maximum Number of 5 People are Allowed in an Illegal or criminal Situation.
30.Robbing anyone to get Stuffs without creating any RP is not allowed.
31.Any misemploy statement or RASP toward Female Players is Strictly not allowed. If the Female player reports a permanent ban will be issued without the chance to appeal.
